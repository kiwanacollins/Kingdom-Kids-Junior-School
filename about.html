About Us

Kingdom Kids Academy - Childcare Centre and Kindergarten

Kingdom Kids Academy is a Pentecostal Christian faith-founded establishment providing family-friendly childcare and highly professional, creative early learning and development services. Our mission is to disciple children for God and instill a positive self-image through modern care and teaching methods. We draw strength from God, as we dedicatedly fear and trust Him, offering quality childcare and preschool services in a safe, nurturing environment.

Located in Katale-Nalumunye, Wakiso district, we serve a diverse community of families, providing easy access from various locations such as Entebbe, Seguku, Natete, and Bunamwaya. Our commitment is to meet the critical need for quality childcare and early childhood learning services in the area.

Core Values

Kingdom Kids Academy operates by seven core values: Love, Honesty, Sincerity, Empathy, Responsibility, Respect for Life, and Creativity. These values guide our everyday decisions, actions, and aspirations, reflecting our culture and commitment to excellence.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to counteract the vulnerability of children in Katale-Nalumunye and surrounding areas by providing a safe, nurturing environment that supports their spiritual, intellectual, and emotional development. Our vision is to lead in providing high-quality childcare and early childhood education, fostering lifelong learning and Christian values.

Philosophy

We believe all children have the right to grow and learn in a safe, loving, and stimulating environment that promotes their unique abilities and creativity. We embrace diversity and are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive community for all families.
